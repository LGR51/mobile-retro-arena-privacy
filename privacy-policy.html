Mobile Retro Arena - Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 11, 2025

Simple Summary: Mobile Retro Arena is completely private. We don't collect, store, or share any personal information.

Information We Collect

Mobile Retro Arena does NOT collect any personal information:

  • No user accounts or registration required
  • No personal data collection
  • No location tracking
  • No contact information
  • No device identifiers

Data Usage and Storage

  • Local Storage Only: Game scores stored locally on your device
  • No Server Communication: No data transmitted to external servers
  • No Cookies: No tracking technologies used
  • No Analytics: No usage tracking or analytics
  • No Advertising: No ads or advertising tracking

Third-Party Services

Mobile Retro Arena does not integrate with any third-party services that collect personal data.

Children's Privacy

Our game is safe for all ages. Since we don't collect any information, there are no privacy concerns for children.
